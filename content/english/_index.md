## Finally Understand Your Personal Finances

A comprehensive Excel workbook that gives you deep insights into your spending, income, assets, and progress toward financial independence.

## Problems This Workbook Solves

### Understand Your Finances
Stop guessing where your money goes. Get detailed insights into your spending patterns, income trends, and net worth growth over time with powerful visualizations and dashboards.

### Own Your Data
Your financial data stays in your control. No subscription lock-in, no cloud dependency. It's an Excel file that you own forever and can modify however you like.

### Flexible & Extensible
Use it as-is or customize it to your needs. Built without macros or VBA, making it accessible to modify and extend. Add your own calculations, categories, or visualizations.

## Designed for Tiller Integration

Seamlessly extends the Tiller Foundation Template, but also works standalone for manual data entry.

## Powerful Features

- **Spending & Income Dashboards** - Analyze spending and income by category and group with interactive slicers and drill-down capability.
- **Net Worth Tracking** - Track your assets, liabilities, and net worth over time with monthly and yearly views.
- **Cashflow Analysis** - Understand your savings rate, spending frequency histogram, and inflation-adjusted trends.
- **Financial Independence Tracking** - Calculate safe withdrawal income and visualize your progress toward financial independence.
- **Year-to-Date Summary** - Get a comprehensive view of your current year finances in one dashboard.
- **No Macros Required** - Built entirely with Excel formulas and pivot tables. No VBA, no scripts, fully transparent.
